Nodebucket application was created by Lates Consulting. It is a small business with approximately 50 employees. We are based in Bellevue Nebraska, which is only 7 miles from Omaha. Although we are based in Bellevue Nebraska, we have an outstanding online presence. We use the latest technology to communicate virtually throughout the life cycle of the project. We will meet in person at critical times during the development and will meet virtually the other times.

We operate within an Agile framework. We use scrum, which has iterations of development and incremental releases to production. What this means is you will see and use portions of the application early in the development process. This way, you can see and use the product as well as give us feedback on the product throughout the project lifecycle. In the Nodebucket application we used 3 sprints to complete the work. We first delivered the sign in and entitlements portion of the application. Then, in sprint 2, we delivered the initial functionality of the task management page where you can add tasks to your task list. Last, in sprint 3, we finalized the application with the ability to move your tasks from ‘to do’ to ‘done’ columns. You can also delete tasks. You can also prioritize the tasks in the ‘to do’ column.

We are a JavaScript shop. We build our applications using JavaScript libraries and frameworks. Our technology stack is Angular for the front end, NodeJs for the middle tier, and MongoDB to store the data. This allows us to only zero in on one skill set, JavaScript, to build our cutting edge applications.
